About Us

"Lets Not Waste" is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting shop owners with consumers who are looking for quality products at discounted prices. Our mission is to provide a solution for the abundance of food that goes to waste every day, especially in the food industry. By enabling shop owners to sell their expiring items online, we are creating a sustainable ecosystem where food waste is minimized, and resources are utilized efficiently. Our goal is to create a win-win scenario for both shop owners and consumers, where the former can maximize their profits, and the latter can enjoy fresh and affordable food. Join us to make a positive impact!
