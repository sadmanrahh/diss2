About Us!

Lets Not Waste is a company helping the rising food waste

and the economy by making people save money on their dinner,

as it is a win/win situation where customers will save money

and shops will earn money off the expiring stock instead of throwing

it away. And we get the satisfaction of helping reduce food waste.
