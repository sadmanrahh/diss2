How to sell items

Reduce food waste + save money

Step 1 - Use "Square.com" to build your payment link by adding the item details and clicking 'Create Payment Link', also remember Square charges 2.6% + 10p per transaction
Step 2 - Use the image address of the image or create one at "imgbb.com"
Step 3 - Go inside LetsNotWaste and from the menu, go to sell page
Step 4 - Click the add item button and fill the required details along with the created payment link and image address
Step 5 - Remember to update the available status to sold when out of stock.

Start Selling!
